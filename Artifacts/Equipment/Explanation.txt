
# Details
Type: The type of equipment, such as clothing, armor, shoes, fashion accessories, shields, etc.
Name: The officially designated name of the equipment.
Common Names: [Optional] Names and titles the equipment may be known by other than its official name.
Tags: Simple tags that help identify the nature of the equipment, such as "Decoration" and "Magic".
Perceived Value: A short description that indicates how inhabitants of the setting would value the equipment.

# Appearance
A description of the equipment's appearance and condition. This can also include the equipment's original appearance, and how it has changed over time.

# Mechanics
Contains information about the equipment's function and mechanics, how it is meant to be used, and any important or special effects it has. If the equipment is magical, this section should include details about the nature of that magic, how the magic works, the limitations of its magic, etc.

# Plot Significance
Contains information about how this equipment is important and relevant to the setting, including details about how it affects those who use it, encounter it, etc. This section should describe conflicts that may result from the introduction, presence, or use of this equipment.

# History
Contains information about the significant and impactful moments of this equipment throughout history, including significant uses by its owners, transformations it has undergone, etc. It may include details about it's creator and the creation process if it is a particularly important story or if the equipment is particularly powerful or renown.
