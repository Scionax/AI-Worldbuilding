
# Details
Type: The type of weapon, such as a sword, a bow, a trident, etc.
Name: The officially designated name of the weapon.
Common Names: [Optional] Names and titles the weapon may be known by other than its official name.
Tags: Simple tags that help identify the nature of the weapon, such as "Defense" and "Magic".
Perceived Value: A short description that indicates how inhabitants of the setting would value the tool.

# Appearance
A description of the weapon's appearance and condition. This can also include the weapon's original appearance, and how it has changed over time.

# Mechanics
Contains information about the weapon's function and mechanics, how it behaves in combat, advantages and disadvantages of the weapon, and any important or special effects it has. If the weapon is magical, this section should include details about the nature of that magic, how the magic works, the limitations of its magic, etc.

# Plot Significance
Contains information about how this weapon is important and relevant to the setting, including details about how it affects those who wield it and those who encounter it. This section should describe conflicts that may result from the introduction, presence, or use of this weapon.

# History
Contains information about the significant and impactful moments of this weapon throughout history, including significant uses through its owners, transformations it has undergone, etc. It may include details about it's creator and the creation process if it is a particularly important story or if the weapon is particularly powerful or renown.
