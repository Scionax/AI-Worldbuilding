
# Details
Type: The type of consumable item, such as a flask of oil, a smoke grenade, a magical scroll, etc.
Name: The officially designated name of the consumable item.
Common Names: [Optional] Names and titles the consumable item may be known by other than its official name.
Tags: Simple tags that help identify the nature of the consumable item, such as "Haste" and "Protection".
Perceived Value: A short description that indicates how inhabitants of the setting would value the consumable item.

# Appearance
A description of the consumable item's appearance and condition. This can also include the consumable item's original appearance, and how it has changed over time.

# Mechanics
Contains information about the consumable item's function and mechanics, how it is meant to be used, and any important or special effects it has. If the consumable item is magical, this section should include details about the nature of that magic, how the magic works, the limitations of its magic, etc.

# Plot Significance
Contains information about how this consumable item is important and relevant to the setting, including details about how it affects those who use it, encounter it, etc. This section should describe conflicts that may result from the introduction, presence, or use of this consumable item.

# History
Contains information about the significant and impactful moments of this consumable item throughout history, including significant uses by its owners, transformations it has undergone, etc. It may include details about it's creator and the creation process if it is a particularly important story or if the consumable item is particularly powerful or renown.
